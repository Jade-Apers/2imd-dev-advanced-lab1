Features of our dilemma game

First feature: The craziest dilemmas

You are presented with endless crazy and difficult dilemmas to solve. You have to think hard because the choice is hard!

Second feature: Choose a category

You are presented with several categories to choose from. These could include: Ethical dilemmas, moral, world, and so on ...

Thirth feature: Play with your friends

You can invite all your friends so you can see each other's answers. Would you think alike or will the answers be different? We are curious!

Fourth feature: View the results

The app collects all the answers from all the players. This way we can measure everything perfectly so we get clear results of which option is preferred by the players.
